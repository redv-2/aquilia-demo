About the label

ELEVATED SOUND FOR
DEEPER MINDS

Aquilia was born from a single conviction: that the most important music exists in the space between categories. We sign artists who create work that cannot be easily filed away.

Founded in 1999 and operating independently across the world, we work with musicians who treat sound as sculpture — shaping it into something that holds weight, texture, and silence in equal measure.

Our catalog spans digital releases, We do not chase trends. We document the edges.
